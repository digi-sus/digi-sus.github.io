Digital Sustainability Global Dialogue for UN Climate Change Conference

We were thrilled to host an exciting and successful event "Digital Sustainability for Climate Change: Global Dialogue", during the United Nations Climate Change Conference in Bonn Germany

Our invited Panelists:
• Naman Kapoor, Chairperson of India-Israel Youth Forum, Emile Boutmy Scholar at Sciences Po
• Dang-Dao Nguyen, Head of ASEAN Delegation to Y20 Indonesia Presidency 22', Yenching Scholar at Peking University
• Jesse Martin, Chairman of the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander International Engagement Organisation
• Sarah M. Kisakye, PhD Student at Communication University of China
• Camila Jiang, TEDxTHU 2023 Speaker, Initiator of "Climate First" Eurasian Youth Union
• Mara Ghilan, Climate Activist & Consultant at DeHavilland, YOUNGO Human Rights Working Group
